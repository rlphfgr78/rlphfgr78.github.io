Research Library

The Lunar Research Initiative maintains an open research library dedicated to the science, engineering, and quality systems needed for safe and reliable lunar manufacturing. Our publications support mission planners, researchers, and industry teams working to develop lunar surface infrastructure.

Featured Publications

A Risk Based Quality Architecture for Lunar Production Systems

A foundational framework that organizes quality and risk across lunar mission tiers and connects mission requirements, critical to quality measures, verification gates, and risk pathways.

Unified Lunar Quality Framework (ULQF) Handbook

A comprehensive guide that links critical to quality measures, predictive signatures, environmental modifiers, and quality gates for lunar surface manufacturing.

Regolith CTQ Capability Reliability Integration

A technical exploration of how regolith properties, process capability indices, and reliability models integrate into a coherent production strategy.

Delay Driven Decision Loops in Lunar Surface Operations

A study of decision making under communication delay and the controls needed to keep operations safe and predictable.
