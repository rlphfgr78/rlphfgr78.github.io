Partnerships

The Lunar Research Initiative collaborates with universities, companies, and agencies that are working to advance lunar manufacturing, quality systems, and surface infrastructure.

Why Partner With LRI

LRI contributes quality engineering, risk modeling, regolith manufacturing insight, and NASA style documentation to collaborative projects.

Types of Partnerships

  • Research collaboration on lunar manufacturing and quality methods.
  • Support for student and academic projects.
  • Technical advisory on quality and risk structures for space hardware.
  • Co development of standards and CTQ libraries.
  • Mission concept support focused on quality and reliability.
