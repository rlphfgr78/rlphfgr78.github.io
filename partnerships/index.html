Partnerships With LRI

LRI works with agencies, companies, universities, and independent researchers who share an interest in the quality and reliability of lunar surface operations. Partnerships focus on practical results that improve mission readiness and reduce risk.

Space Agencies and Programs

Collaboration on quality frameworks, regolith manufacturing standards, and verification strategies for lunar infrastructure projects. LRI provides independent analysis and technical memoranda that programs can adapt into their internal documentation.

Industry Partners

Engagement with companies developing lunar construction systems, materials, and support equipment. Joint work can include process capability studies, CTQ development, and predictive quality architectures for product lines.

Universities and Students

Opportunities for graduate and undergraduate projects that connect academic research with real quality problems in lunar manufacturing. LRI can support thesis topics, design projects, and shared datasets.

Starting a Conversation

To explore a partnership, share a short summary of your mission concept, technology, or research area and describe how you would use lunar quality frameworks. LRI will respond with potential collaboration paths or suggestions for next steps.
