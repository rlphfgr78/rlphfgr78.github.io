Partnerships

The Lunar Research Initiative collaborates with universities, companies, government agencies, and nonprofit organizations working to advance lunar manufacturing, quality systems, and surface infrastructure. Our partnerships bring scientific rigor, structured frameworks, and quality engineering methods into research programs, technology demonstrations, and mission planning.

Why Partner With LRI

LRI provides expertise in lunar quality engineering, risk modeling, regolith manufacturing systems, predictive data structures, and NASA style documentation. Our frameworks and technical memoranda support reliable, safe, and scalable lunar operations.

For organizations building technology for the Moon, LRI adds structure, verification logic, and assurance methods that increase mission confidence and reduce development risk.

Types of Partnerships

  • Research Collaboration: Joint studies, modeling work, and development of lunar manufacturing techniques.
  • Student and Academic Support: Capstone support, research guidance, and access to technical frameworks.
  • Technical Advisory: Quality, verification, and risk advisory support for engineering teams.
  • Co-developed Standards: Joint development of technical frameworks, CTQ libraries, and verification models.
  • Mission Concept Support: Assistance in shaping quality and risk structures for early-phase mission planning.

Example Collaboration Pathways

University Laboratory: A lunar additive manufacturing lab integrates ULQF into its process characterization studies.

Research Consortium: Multiple research groups adopt LRI’s verification templates for regolith system experiments.

Industry Partner: A space hardware startup consults with LRI to build risk-driven quality gates for lunar equipment.

Mission Planning Team: A mission architecture group applies LRI frameworks to improve reliability of surface operations.

How to Begin a Partnership

If your organization is interested in collaborating, the first step is to reach out with a brief summary of your project, research goals, or mission concept. LRI will respond with a customized collaboration path based on your needs.
