LRI Research Initiatives

LRI organizes its scientific work into four core program areas that support lunar manufacturing quality, predictive modeling, and regolith based production.

Lunar Manufacturing Quality Architecture

Defines mission tier quality systems and risk structures for surface infrastructure.

Regolith Manufacturing Systems

Studies regolith feedstock, mechanical behavior, process capability, and reliability for lunar routes.

Predictive Quality and Decision Support

Develops predictive signatures, drift detection, and time shifted decision logic for lunar operations.

Lunar Standards and Verification

Creates open frameworks, CTQ libraries, and verification structures for lunar production systems.
