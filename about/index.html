About the Lunar Research Initiative

Our Mission

The Lunar Research Initiative (LRI) advances scientific knowledge and public understanding of lunar surface manufacturing. We develop models, tools, and standards that improve the quality, reliability, and safety of lunar operations. Our work supports responsible exploration and helps establish a strong technical foundation for long-duration presence on the Moon.

Our Vision

Humanity will establish the first generation of lunar infrastructure with standards that reflect scientific rigor, predictable performance, and mission safety. LRI envisions a lunar economy supported by transparent quality frameworks, validated manufacturing methods, and data-driven decision structures.

Our Values

Scientific rigor: We base all work on evidence and transparent methodology.

Public benefit: We strengthen safe and responsible lunar exploration.

Open access: We share frameworks and standards with the research community.

Mission safety: We prioritize reliability and risk awareness in every recommendation.

Education: We support learning and technical readiness for future lunar professionals.

Research Divisions

Quality and Reliability Research Division: Predictive quality, CTQs, reliability systems.

Lunar Manufacturing Systems Division: Regolith processing, additive manufacturing, environmental loads.

Data and Modeling Division: Simulation tools, predictive models, AI-assisted decision support.

Standards and Documentation Division: NASA-style memos, technical frameworks, LRI handbooks.

Outreach and Partnerships Division: University and industry collaboration, education, public engagement.
