About the Lunar Research Initiative

Our Mission

The Lunar Research Initiative (LRI) advances scientific knowledge and public understanding of lunar surface manufacturing. We develop models, tools, and standards that improve the quality, reliability, and safety of lunar operations.

Our Vision

LRI envisions a lunar economy supported by transparent quality frameworks, validated manufacturing methods, and data driven decision structures.

Our Values

Scientific rigor: Evidence based methods and transparent reasoning.

Public benefit: Support for responsible and safe exploration.

Open access: Sharing frameworks and tools with the research community.

Mission safety: Reliability and risk awareness in every recommendation.

Education: Support for future lunar professionals.
