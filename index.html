Lunar Research Initiative is a nonprofit scientific and educational organization focused on the next generation of lunar infrastructure.

What is the Lunar Research Initiative

The Lunar Research Initiative (LRI) develops the scientific foundations that future lunar missions need to build safe and reliable infrastructure on the Moon. We combine quality engineering, reliability science, and lunar manufacturing research to create methods that mission planners can trust.

What We Do

Quality Architectures

Designing mission tier quality structures that connect requirements, risk, and verification for lunar systems.

Regolith Manufacturing Research

Studying regolith based processes, capability, and reliability for construction and production on the lunar surface.

Predictive Quality

Developing predictive signatures, drift detection, and decision structures for time shifted lunar operations.

Standards and Frameworks

Publishing open frameworks like ULQF that help unify quality practices across lunar missions and organizations.
